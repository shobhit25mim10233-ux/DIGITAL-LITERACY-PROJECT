Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
✅ Prevention Tips
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts; enable 2FA wherever possible.
Never share OTPs, passwords, or UPI PINs with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
Always verify unknown links, emails, or messages before clicking.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or personal accounts.
Download apps only from official sources like Play Store or App Store.
Keep your phone and apps updated to the latest versions.
Log out from shared/public computers after use.
Enable screen lock (PIN, fingerprint, or face lock) on your devices.
💰 UPI / Financial Safety
Check carefully if it is a “Pay” or “Collect Request” before entering your UPI PIN.
Never scan a QR code to receive money—QR codes are only for making payments.
🚨 Report Cyber Fraud
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
